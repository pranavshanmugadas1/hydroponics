Hydroponics

Hydroponics is the best upcoming field of farming. It has yielded great success which no one has capitalized on with automation. Yet. This is what we intend to do with our hydroponics robot. Our robot uses several tools such as IOT (Internet Of Things) and ML (Machine Learning) to provide individual care to crops so that you can yield the perfect harvest. Similar to our traditional farming robot, we intend to use swarm robotics to systematically cultivate plants and other crops to perfection. We can achieve this by using a combination of machine vision to detect the overall health of the plants and provide the necessary nourishments such as our concoction of citric and carbonic acids for obtaining the optimum pH level required for the crops. Also, our Potassium Nitrate solution enriches the plant with proteins as Potassium Nitrate is the most efficient source of potassium.

Why Choose Us?

So, why choose us when there are plenty of other farming tools you could use?
Through a rigorous amount of research, we found that if our product becomes a full-fledged instrument, it can offer a 40% decrease in maintainance and labour costs, while also offering a 21% increase in plant growth as it gives individual attention to each plant.
